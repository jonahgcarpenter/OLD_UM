Batman

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is from DC Comics. Batman is inferior in power compared to the rest of the Justice League but maintains his status as a founder and leader with his detective skills. Although he can hold his own in combat when needed, Batman relies on his intellect. He first appeared in Detective Comics #27 but is best portrayed by Christian Bale in Christopher Nolan's 3-film series.

  • He is highly intelligent and uses his detective skills to solve complex problems and crimes.
  • Despite having no superpowers, he relies more on his enemies' fear and his skills of deception to outsmart opponents.
  • As a firm believer in the justice system, he seeks to imprison his foes and rejects killing at all costs.
